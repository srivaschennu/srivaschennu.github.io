Funding

 The UK Engineering and Physical Sciences Research Council funds our MOHAWK project on developing A Computational Prototype for Electroencephalographic Brain Connectomics.
The Evelyn Trust funds BEdside Test of Awareness in Disorders of Consciousness (BETADOC) project hosted at the University of Cambridge.
The NIHR Brain Injury Healthcare Technology Cooperative provided funding for high-density EEG nets we used to assess vegetative and minimally conscious patients.
The Royal Society funded a UK-India joint symposium on translational neuroscience applications of my research.
